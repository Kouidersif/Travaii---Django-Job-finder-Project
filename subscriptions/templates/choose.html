How do I cancel my subscription?
Note that once your subscription is cancelled, your Travaii Premium Features will remain active until the end of the period you have already paid for,

You can cancel your subscription at any time by following the instructions below

  • Log in to your Travaii account to access account settings .
  • Select Manage Subscription from the menu.
  • Click Cancel Subscription.
  • Follow the cancellation steps.
  • Once your subscription is cancelled, you will have access to all Travaii content until the end of the paid period.
